World Wide Web

The World Wide Web(WWW) is a collection of digital pages. It contains information on different topics and made available through internet. These pages may include texts, graphics, audio and video files it can be accessed easily by using internet facility

The information presented on these pages is made attractive for viewers. The pages, which collectively form World Wide Web are known as web pages
